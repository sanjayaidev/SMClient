Welcome to Graphicy Media, your all-in-one scheduling and automation platform for Meta's ecosystem — Instagram, Facebook, and Threads. We're on a mission to help creators, marketers, and businesses save time, grow their audience, and engage meaningfully with their community.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Graphicy Media was born from a simple observation: managing multiple social media platforms is overwhelming. Between scheduling posts, replying to comments, and analyzing performance, creators spend more time on logistics than on creating. We built Content Planner to change that.

What We Offer

  • Smart Scheduling — Plan and schedule posts across Instagram, Facebook, and Threads from one unified calendar.
  • AI-Powered Automation — Build custom reply workflows with keyword triggers, AI responses, and intelligent fallbacks.
  • Multi-Platform Management — Connect all your Meta accounts and manage them seamlessly.
  • Content Optimization — Schedule posts at the right time, track performance, and refine your strategy.
