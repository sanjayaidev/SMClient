Data Deletion Request

Your data, your choice. We're here to help.

At Graphicy Media, we respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your account and associated data from Content Planner.

📌 Quick Summary: You can delete your account and all associated data by submitting a request via email or through the in-app deletion feature. We will process your request within 30 days.

What We Delete

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, and profile details.
  • Content Data: All scheduled posts, drafts, captions, images, and videos.
  • Automation Data: All workflows, rules, replies, and logs associated with your account.
  • Connection Data: Your connected Meta platform tokens and permissions.
  • Usage Data: Analytics, activity logs, and interaction history.

What We Retain

Certain data may be retained for legal or operational purposes:

  • Billing History: Transaction records may be retained for accounting and tax compliance (up to 7 years as required by law).
  • Logs: Anonymized logs may be retained for security and fraud prevention purposes.
  • Backups: Residual data may persist in backup systems for up to 30 days after deletion, during which it is not accessible.

How to Request Deletion

You can request deletion in two ways:

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Log in to your Content Planner account.
  2. Navigate to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  3. Click "Request Account Deletion".
  4. Confirm your request. You will receive a confirmation email.

Option 2: Email Request

  1. Send an email to graphicyin@gmail.com with the subject line: "DATA DELETION REQUEST".
  2. Include the following in your email:
    • Your full name
    • Your registered email address
    • Your phone number (optional, for verification)
    • A confirmation that you wish to delete your account
  3. We may contact you to verify your identity before processing.

What Happens After Your Request

  • Acknowledgment: We will acknowledge your request within 48 hours.
  • Processing: We will begin the deletion process immediately.
  • Completion: Your data will be fully deleted within 30 days.
  • Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.
⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you change your mind after requesting deletion, you must contact us within 24 hours to cancel the request.

Things to Consider Before Deleting

  • Permanent Action: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Loss of Access: You will lose access to all scheduled posts, automations, and account connections.
  • Meta Platforms: Deleting your account here does not delete data from Meta Platforms (Instagram, Facebook, Threads). You must delete content directly on those platforms if desired.
  • Pending Activities: Any scheduled posts will be canceled. Automations will stop immediately.
